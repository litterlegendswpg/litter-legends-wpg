Why this exists

Because "someone" is actually us

Winnipeg is beautiful and also... weirdly full of coffee lids, plastic bags, and that one shoe. Litter Legends WPG started as simple garbage walks‚Äîjust heading out with a grabber and a bag and seeing what happens.

  • Litter attracts more litter. Clean corners tend to stay cleaner.
  • Five minutes on your normal route is more realistic than a giant cleanup once a year.
  • You don't need permission to care about your own block.

Trash talk (the good kind)

Tips for safe & non-awkward cleanup

Safety first

  • Avoid broken glass, needles, or anything sharp or sketchy‚Äîcall 311 if needed.
  • Stay visible: bright clothing or a small light if you're near traffic.
  • Don't climb into ditches, fences, or icy riverbanks just for a stray can.
  • Wash or sanitize your hands when you're done.

Social comfort

  • If someone asks what you're doing: "Just doing a quick garbage walk on my route." That's it.
  • Headphones are allowed. Awkward eye contact is optional.
  • It's okay to do a tiny section and leave the rest for another day.
